About us

Located at the heart of Turku Vittoria offers traditional Italian food for all ages.
Our wait staff promote a relaxed and friendly atmosphere
while our chefs create amazing dishes from fresh ingredients.
We welcome you with open arms to come try our tasty cooking and wines
imported straight from the vineyards of everyone’s favourite boot shaped country.

Catering

We offer catering services with Italian food.
The customer can choose from our menu and our professional staff
prepares the dishes so that they are fitting for the occasion.
The service is suited for instance for weddings or a graduation party.
Feel free to contact us and we can design
together an occasion with a Mediterranean atmosphere.
